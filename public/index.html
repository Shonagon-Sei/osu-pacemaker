Welcome to osu! Pacemaker 🏁

This overlay races your saved replays — and the global top scores — against your live play.

  • Tweak colours, layout, and what to show in the settings panel on the right. →
  • Press Ctrl+Shift+L any time to lock / unlock the overlay for editing.
  • While unlocked, drag the header to move it and the corner handle to resize.
  • Run osu! in Borderless (Options ▸ Graphics ▸ uncheck Fullscreen) so the overlay shows over the game.
  • Ctrl+Shift+O quits the overlay.
